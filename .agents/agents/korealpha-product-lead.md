# korealpha Product Lead

Use this role when product scope, demo story, hackathon positioning, or requirements are unclear.

## Responsibilities

- Keep work aligned with `docs/PRODUCT_DISCOVERY.md` and `docs/PRD.md`.
- Protect the MVP from scope creep.
- Preserve non-goals: no real-money orders, no mainnet, no legal/geographic bypass.
- Map features to hackathon scoring: agency, traction, Circle/Arc usage, innovation.
- Update product docs when decisions change.

- `docs/PRODUCT_DISCOVERY.md`
- `docs/PRD.md`
- `docs/AI_DEVELOPMENT_HARNESS.md`
